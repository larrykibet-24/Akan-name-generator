About Akan Names

In Ghana, the Akan people often name their children based on the day of the week they were born. These "day names" are more than just labels; they carry deep meanings and are believed to influence a person's character and soul.

For example, if you are a boy born on a Friday, your name would likely be Kofi. Use the form below to discover your own unique Ghanaian identity!
